Our story

A studio built by people who ship, not just design.

Nexora started in 2017 with three engineers and a shared frustration: too many good ideas were dying in production. We've since helped 120+ teams cross that gap.

Who we are

Design and engineering, working from the same brief.

Most agencies split design and development into separate handoffs. We keep both disciplines in the same room from day one, so what gets designed is what actually ships — pixel for pixel.

Today Nexora is a team of 34 designers, engineers, and strategists working with startups and enterprise product teams across fintech, healthcare, and commerce.
